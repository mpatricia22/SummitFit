About us



WE ARE A NETWORK OF GYMS WITH THE BEST COST-BENEFIT IN IRELAND

Our units offer a team dedicated to helping you with whatever it takes and transforming your goal into great results. You will train in large, modern gyms with the latest equipment, classes with gymnastics, dance and fights (zumba, jump, functional, pilates, jiu-jitsu, muay thai, among others), which are renewed throughout the year with seasonal modalities, and instructors at your availability to help your training. We are proud to offer a premium service at an affordable price, after all, easy access to physical activity can change anyone's life. Join the SummitFitteam and discover the gym you've always wanted!


Our mission

To enhance the quality of life in the communities, we serve through our fitness philosophy, facilities, programs, and products and to instill in the lives of people everywhere the value of health and fitness.

Our history

SummitFit was born in 2022! Dublin opened in January,2022, followed by the first location outside Dublin,  Cork City in May 2022! SummitFit is the first commercial gym to have a heated chamber. The heated chamber is now available in Dublin & Cork too.At SummitFit we're here to provide a unique environment in which anyone – and we mean anyone – can be comfortable. A diverse, Judgement Free Zone® where a lasting, active lifestyle can be built. Our product is a tool, a means to an end; not a brand name or a mold-maker, but a tool that can be used by anyone. In the end, it’s all about you. As we evolve and educate ourselves, we will seek to perfect this safe, energetic environment, where everyone feels accepted and respected. We are not here to kiss your butt, only to kick it if that’s what you need.
